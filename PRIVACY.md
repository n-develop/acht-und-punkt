# Privacy Policy — 8 und Punkt!

*Last updated: June 1, 2026*

## Overview

8 und Punkt! is a local score-tracking app for the card game "8 und aus!". This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information - in short: it doesn't collect any.

## Data Collection

**8 und Punkt! does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The app does not:

- collect your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers
- access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device sensor
- use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs of any kind
- display advertisements
- connect to the internet
- create or require a user account

## Data Storage

The only data the app works with is the player names and scores you enter during a game session. This information exists exclusively in the app's memory while the app is running and is never written to disk, synced to iCloud, or transmitted anywhere. When you close the app or start a new game, that data is gone.

## Third-Party Services

8 und Punkt! uses no third-party libraries, SDKs, or services. There are no analytics providers, advertising networks, or crash-reporting tools integrated into the app.

## Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13 (COPPA) and children under 16 (GDPR).

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes - for example because a future version of the app adds a feature that involves data - this document will be updated and the "Last updated" date at the top will reflect that change.
